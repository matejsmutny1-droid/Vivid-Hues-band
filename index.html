ABOUT

Vivid Hues is a genre blending duo based in Sweden creating nostalgic soundscapes for the heart and the subconscious. Formed by Slovak artists Matej Smutny and Juraj Oravec the project explores their own musical language which they call colorful electro pop. Their music blends electronics with live instruments including drums electric guitars and bass while their performances use loopers samples and layered textures.
