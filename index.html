Vivid Hues is a genre blending duo based in Sweden creating nostalgic soundscapes for the heart and the subconscious. Formed by Slovak artists Matej Smutny and Juraj Oravec the project explores their own musical language which they call colorful electro pop.

Their music blends electronics with live instruments such as drums electric guitars and bass. On stage they recreate their sound using loopers samples and layered textures.
