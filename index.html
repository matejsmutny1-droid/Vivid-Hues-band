Vivid Hues is a genre blending duo based in Sweden creating nostalgic soundscapes for the heart and the subconscious. The project was formed by Slovak artists Matej Smutny and Juraj Oravec as a space to explore their own musical language which they call colorful electro pop.

Their music blends electronic textures with live instruments including drums electric guitars and bass. In live performances they rebuild their sound using loopers samples and layered textures turning each show into an evolving sonic landscape.

With swirling synths ambient grooves and vulnerable melodies Vivid Hues drifts between dream pop alt R&B and lo fi psychedelia. Their music feels cinematic yet intimate shaped somewhere between city lights and open sea.
